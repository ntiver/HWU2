SUCCESS STORIES

Wildlife Preservation Society of Queensland

The Wildlife Preservation Society of Queensland is the longest-running and one of the most respected wildlife conservation organisations in Queensland. The Wildlife Queensland Brisbane Branch advocates conservation through a diverse educational program, which includes expert speakers at monthly public meetings. Hear With Us first introduced captioning to address potential communication barriers with a foreign guest speaker. These monthly public speaker events are now captioned live as a testament to the benefits of increased engagement and attendance by the public.

Donald Simpson Community Centre

The Donald Simpson Community Centre welcomes members of all ages, especially elderly people, to participate in various activities run daily. While the centre is equipped with hearing loops, the introduction of captioning at one event sparked member awareness and discussion of the benefits of a text equivalent. Real-time captioning is now regularly included in presentations and can be requested at the centre.


Wet 'n' Wild and Cineplex Victoria Point

Open captioned movies are being shown at Cineplex Victoria Point.
