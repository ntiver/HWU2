CAPTIONS
MATTER

Captions are a visual communication tool and aid for your message.

FOR PEOPLE –– FOR EVENTS – FOR MEETINGS - FOR PRESENTATIONS - FOR YOU

Communication matters.  Communication is all around us.  Communication is at the root of what makes us human, and a core part of our society.  In our daily lives, communication helps us connect to others, build relationships, enjoy experiences.

Business, conferences, meetings, festivals, travel, entertainments, functions, fundraising activities and ceremonies are important parts of the lives of many people of all ages.  This includes people with various degrees of hearing, audio processing disorders, dyslexia, attention and focus issues.

Venues that offer year-round public engagements and events such as transport, sporting venues, entertainment venues, amusement parks and shopping centres are often not communication accessible.  This lack of access to information means that some people are unable to participate equally in these situations unless specific provisions are made.  Communication is only effective if the receiver understands the message.

Including accurate captions makes the experience more enjoyable and accessible so that more people may engage with your important messages.
